About

Interested in the "why," not just the prediction

I'm a Data Analyst with an M.Sc. in Data Science from the University of Europe for Applied Sciences, Germany. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and human-centered design — I care less about squeezing out another percentage point of accuracy and more about whether a person can actually trust and act on what a model tells them.

My thesis tested that idea directly: I built parallel static and interactive explanation pipelines for the same models, then ran a real user study to see which one people understood faster and trusted more. The interactive version won — measurably, not just anecdotally.

Based in Halle (Saale), Germany. Open to data analyst and ML/analytics roles.
